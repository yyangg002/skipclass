About CampusFlash HK

CampusFlash HK is designed to connect university students with nearby SMEs through quick, location-based offers.

Our mission

We help students save money and help small businesses attract more customers, especially during slower hours.

Why it is useful

  • Students get easy access to affordable food, drinks, printing, and study supplies.
  • SMEs can create limited-time promotions and increase local visibility.
  • Food rescue offers can reduce waste near campus.

Key features

  • Multi-language interface
  • Campus-based filtering
  • Merchant flash deal creator
  • Claim tracking with local storage

Prototype note

This version is a frontend-ready prototype. It works as a static website and stores demo data locally in your browser.
